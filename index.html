HDFC Mobile Banking App
UX/UI Redesign

“All project designs were approved by clients and tailored to their specific needs, which may differ from standard design trends or other designers' personal styles.”
The HDFC mobile banking app redesign was a self-initiated case study aimed at solving core usability pain points in one of India’s most widely used financial apps. My focus was on improving accessibility, simplifying navigation, and creating a clean visual hierarchy for a smoother user experience.
I restructured key flows like account summary, fund transfers, and bill payments to reduce cognitive load and improve clarity. The result? A modern, user friendly interface that feels less like a maze and more like a conversation.
